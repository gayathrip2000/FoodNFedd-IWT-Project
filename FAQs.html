Frequently Asked Questions

What is FoodnFeed.com?

FoodnFeed is a food delivery platform that makes it possible for customers to have great food from their favorite restaurants delivered right to their home. The process is fast and efficient and easy.

Is FoodnFeed.com user friendly?

Yes. All you need to do is go to the website on your phone/laptop and add your delivery address. You will then be able to access a full menu of all the best restaurants in your city. Just select the food you wish to have delivered and check in the app to track how soon it will arrive.

What payment methods does FoodnFeed.com accept?

FoodnFeed.com accepts Visa, MasterCard, American Express,Debit Cards, PayPal and also Cash on Delivery Available...

Can I make changes to my order?

Most of the time, you may ask for modifications to your order. To do this, just make use of the contact Customer Support field for any request.

What is FoodnFeed.com' Cancelation Policy?

FoodnFeed.com cancelation policy will be dependent on the status of the order. Canceling an order before it has been accepted by the restaurant will entitle you to a full refund. Canceling after the order has been accepted by the restaurant will entitle you to a partial refund.

How can be a Member of FoodnFeed.com ?

Click on 'Login & Sign up' at the top of the page. Then fill out your information in the 'Create an account' section and click the 'Sign Up' button. You can also create an account directly after placing an order. Your delivery address and the order details will then be saved in your account.

How can I place the order via FoodnFeed.com ?

Enter your exact delivery address, payment method and your phone number. Always make sure that you enter the correct phone number to help us contact you regarding your order if needed. Now sit back, relax, and we'll get your food delivered to your doorstep
